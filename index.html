For Bubu
A love letter for Chubby
✿
In the golden hour
She smells of spring
Where stories are written

In every quiet moment — in golden restaurant light,
in the soft warmth of a car with flowers in your hands,
in the colour of the world behind you —

you are the most beautiful scene
I have ever been lucky enough to witness.

You carry magic without knowing it.
The way you lose yourself in little moments, the way you hold a bouquet
like the flowers were always meant for you —
they were.

Every day beside you feels like a love film:
unhurried, luminous, worth remembering forever.

You are my favourite story, Bubu.
And I would choose you in every one.

With all the love in every sky
Always yours ✦
